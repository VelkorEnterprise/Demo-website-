## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is this really free?
**A:** Yes! Completely free, no ads, no watermarks, no sign-up required.

### Q: How many memes can I create?
**A:** Unlimited! Create as many as you want.

### Q: Do I need an account?
**A:** Nope! Everything works in your browser.

### Q: Where are my memes saved?
**A:** Downloads go to your default download folder. Saved memes are stored in your browser's IndexedDB.

### Q: Can I use my own images?
**A:** The current version uses built-in templates. Custom uploads coming soon!

### Q: Does it work offline?
**A:** Partially. Once loaded, the editor works offline, but loading new templates requires internet.

### Q: Why aren't some images loading?
**A:** Some placeholder images are generated. Use search to find the 25 real popular templates!

### Q: Can I edit saved memes?
**A:** Yes! Access your saved memes from the library view.

### Q: Is my data private?
**A:** Yes! Everything happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

### Q: What browsers are supported?
**A:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions). Canvas and IndexedDB required.
